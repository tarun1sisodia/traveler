It operates as an end-to-end travel solution provider, including flight bookings, hotel reservations, holiday packages, and more. It serves as a one-stop destination for all travel needs.

3. Medical reasons to cancel your domestic travel, and receive up to INR3500 compensation against airline cancellation charges per person per segment.


How to create an account on MyTour?

For all your travel bookings, sign up on MyTour.

1. Go to the home page, click the link (top right), and enter your email address. We'll then send you an account activation link.
2. Click it, and you'll be able to access your MyTour account.

The account activation link expires three days after you provide us with your email address, so please activate your account within that time frame.

How to search for flights on MyTour?

We make it relatively simple to search and book MyTour Flights

1. Enter source and destination cities/airports.
2. Select the date of travel.
3. Select the number of travellers.
4.Hit enter, and you're done! You have a search list of all available flights, sorted by price.

You may also refine your hotel booking search results by utilising parameters such as price, star rating, traveller rating, facilities, and preferences such as hill-view or couple-friendly hotels. MyTour offers hotels for all types of stays. MyTour hotels are curated to give you the best value for your money.



How to search for hotels on MyTour?

Booking a hotel online has never been easier than it is with MyTour.

1. Click the 'hotels' tab on the site.
2. Enter the city/locality/landmark/hotel name in the search bar.
3. Enter the check-in and check-out dates.
4. Choose the number of travellers and press enter.

You may also refine your hotel booking search results by utilising parameters such as price, star rating, traveller rating, facilities, and preferences such as hill-view or couple-friendly hotels. MyTour offers hotels for all types of stays. MyTour hotels are curated to give you the best value for your money.



How to book train tickets on MyTour?

MyTour enables you to buy train tickets and search for them. Simply click 'check availability' on the search results page, and you will find the book button to click for bookings. Booking MyTour trains is simple and fast.
